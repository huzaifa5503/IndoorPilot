What We Do

People are relying on their electronic devices to solve some of their common day to day problems.Since the use of specialized emitters and sensors for navigating indoors was questionable as it was not scalable and quite expensive, so there had to be an alternative to solve this issue.

Idea

It happens all the time that once you have reached your destination with the help of Google Maps, for example a university, you are lost and do not know where to go and this is where Google Maps cannot help you.

Concept

Indoor Pilot is a software that solves the problem of indoor positioning using Wireless access points (Wi-Fi).

Design

The reason for this is that GPS technology relies on satellite signals. The strength of these satellite signals is disrupted by the thick walls of buildings and thus access inside the building is difficult

Develop

Specifically, Wi-Fi fingerprinting will be used to estimate indoor coordinates of connected terminals with no additional infrastructure required and the compass direction will be detected using trilateration.
